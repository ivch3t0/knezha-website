Cultural Festival Coming This August

Date: July 5, 2025

Knezha will celebrate its annual cultural festival this August, where the streets will be filled with music, dancing, traditional costumes, and local food. The event brings together families, performers, and artisans from all over Bulgaria.

Highlights include: traditional folk performances, workshops for children, local cuisine, and a craft bazaar. Join us for a celebration of community and tradition!
