Local Football Tournament Excites the Town

Date: July 8, 2025

The annual football tournament in Knezha brought together 12 local teams for a day full of energy and sportsmanship. The central stadium was filled with fans cheering for their favorite teams.

The mayor awarded the winning team with a trophy and prizes. Youth teams also played in the morning, creating a family-friendly atmosphere. Stay tuned for more events like this!
