Knezha Opens New Eco Park

Date: July 10, 2025

A brand-new eco park has opened in the town center, offering residents a peaceful place to relax, walk, and enjoy nature. The park includes benches, walking paths, and a children’s playground.

The project was funded by the municipality and aims to promote green living. Visitors can also enjoy art installations and a small café within the park area.
